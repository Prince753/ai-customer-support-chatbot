## Size & Fit Guide - Earbuds

### Choosing the Right Ear Tips:
1. **Small (S)**: For smaller ear canals
2. **Medium (M)**: Most common fit, included by default
3. **Large (L)**: For larger ear canals

### Testing Fit:
- Earbuds should sit securely without falling
- No pain or pressure after 30 minutes
- ANC effectiveness depends on proper seal
- If unsure, try one size up and one size down

### Care Instructions:
- Clean ear tips weekly with dry cloth
- Avoid moisture in charging case
- Store in case when not in use
- Do not expose to extreme temperatures

## Common Questions

**Q: How do I pair the headphones with my phone?**

**Q: Can I use these for calls?**

**Q: What's the difference between ANC and Transparency mode?**
A: ANC blocks outside noise for immersive listening. Transparency mode lets outside sounds in so you can hear announcements or conversations.
