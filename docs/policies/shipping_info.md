# TechStore Shipping Information

## 1. Shipping Options

### Standard Shipping
- **Cost**: Free on orders above ₹999, otherwise ₹99
- **Delivery Time**: 
  - Metro cities: 3-5 business days
  - Tier 2 cities: 5-7 business days
  - Other areas: 7-10 business days

### Express Shipping
- **Cost**: ₹199 (free on orders above ₹2999)
- **Delivery Time**:
  - Metro cities: 1-2 business days
  - Tier 2 cities: 2-3 business days
- **Available**: Select pin codes only

### Same-Day Delivery
- **Cost**: ₹299
- **Availability**: Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad
- **Order by**: 12 PM for same-day delivery
- **Delivery window**: 6 PM - 10 PM

## 2. Shipping Partners

We work with trusted courier partners:

- FedEx (for international)

## 3. Tracking Your Order

### How to Track:
1. **Website**: Log in → My Orders → Click order → View Tracking
2. **Email**: Check your shipping confirmation email for tracking link
3. **SMS**: Reply TRACK to any order update SMS
4. **AI Chat**: Share your order ID with our support bot

### Tracking Statuses:
- **Order Placed**: Order confirmed and payment received
- **Processing**: Order is being packed
- **Shipped**: Handed over to courier partner
- **In Transit**: On the way to your location
- **Out for Delivery**: Will be delivered today
- **Delivered**: Successfully delivered

## 4. Delivery Information

### Delivery Attempts:
- 3 delivery attempts will be made
- If undelivered, package returns to origin
- Re-shipment charges may apply

### Delivery Requirements:
- Someone must be present to receive the package
- Valid ID may be required for high-value orders
- OTP verification for certain orders

### Safe Drop:
- Available in select areas
- Package left at doorstep if no one is home
- Photo proof of delivery provided

## 5. International Shipping

### Available Countries:
- USA, UK, Canada, Australia, UAE, Singapore
- More countries available on request

### International Shipping Rates:
- Calculated at checkout based on weight and destination
- Customs and import duties are buyer's responsibility

### International Delivery Time:
- Standard: 10-15 business days
- Express: 5-7 business days

## 6. Shipping Restrictions

### Items with Restrictions:
- Batteries shipped only by ground
- Hazardous materials have special handling
- Large items may have additional delivery time

### PIN Code Serviceability:
- Check serviceability at checkout
- Some remote areas may have limited options

## 7. Holiday Season Shipping

During peak seasons (Diwali, Christmas, etc.):
- Expect 2-3 extra days for delivery
- Order early to ensure timely delivery
- Express shipping recommended for time-sensitive gifts

## 8. Common Questions

**Q: Why is my package delayed?**
A: Delays can occur due to weather, festivals, or high volume. Track your package for updates or contact support.

**Q: Can I change my delivery address?**
A: Yes, before shipping. Contact support immediately after placing order.

**Q: What if I miss my delivery?**
A: Courier will attempt 2 more times. You can also reschedule via tracking link.
