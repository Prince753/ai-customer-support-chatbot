# TechStore Return & Refund Policy

## 1. Return Policy Overview

At TechStore, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer hassle-free returns within 30 days of delivery.

## 2. Eligibility for Returns

### Products Eligible for Return:
- Unused items in original packaging
- Items with all original tags and accessories
- Products without any damage or signs of use
- Items purchased within the last 30 days

### Products NOT Eligible for Return:
- Customized or personalized items
- Opened software or digital products
- Intimate or sanitary items
- Perishable goods
- Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"

## 3. Return Process

### Step 1: Initiate Return
1. Log in to your TechStore account
2. Navigate to "My Orders"
3. Select the order containing the item
4. Click "Request Return"
5. Select the reason for return

### Step 2: Pack Your Item
- Use original packaging if possible
- Include all accessories and documentation
- Secure the package properly

### Step 3: Ship or Schedule Pickup
- **Free Pickup**: Available in most metro cities
- **Drop-off**: Use our prepaid shipping label at nearest courier partner

### Step 4: Receive Refund
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item
- Original payment method will be credited

## 4. Refund Policy

### Full Refund:
- Unused items returned in original packaging within 30 days
- Defective products (verified by our team)
- Wrong item shipped

### Partial Refund (70-90%):
- Items with opened packaging but unused
- Minor cosmetic damage to packaging
- Missing non-essential accessories

### No Refund:
- Items damaged by customer
- Products used or worn
- Items past 30-day return window
- Non-returnable categories

## 5. Refund Timeline

| Payment Method | Refund Timeline |
|----------------|-----------------|
| UPI | 1-2 business days |
| Credit/Debit Card | 5-7 business days |
| Net Banking | 5-7 business days |
| Wallet | 24 hours |
| Cash on Delivery | 5-7 business days (to bank account) |

## 6. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective product:
1. Report within 48 hours of delivery
2. Provide photos of the damage
3. We'll arrange free replacement or full refund
4. No need to return the damaged item in most cases

## 7. Exchange Policy

- Exchanges available within 7 days of delivery
- Same product in different size/color only
- Subject to availability
- No additional shipping charges
